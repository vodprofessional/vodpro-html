VUIX Online Video Product Development 2015

Conference / Exhibition
October 10th, 2015 11:00 - 18:00 (GMT)
This event has already happened!

VOD Professional is delighted to invite you to VUIX 2015 - our conference dedicated to online video product development, user interfaces and user experience. Co-hosted by Brightcove and taking place at London's beautiful Kensington Roof Gardens, leading broadcasters and technology suppliers will be discussing their insights for building successful VOD services across a range of platforms.

We've got a great set of speakers lined up from BBC Worldwide, ITV, Ostmodern, Fan TV and Hopster. There's also a very special panel on content recommendation made up of five leading industry vendors who'll tell us how discovery and personalisation functionality will influence UIs over the next few years.
